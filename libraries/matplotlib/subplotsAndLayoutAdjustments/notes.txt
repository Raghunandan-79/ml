What is Subplot ?
    linechart(sales)
    barchart(expenses)

    above both must be on same canvas

Why they are useful ?
    1. Compare multiple charts side-by-side
    2. Space reduction
    3. Quickly analyze mostly used in EDA

1. plt.subplot():
    a. Grid Layout
    b. Quick Creation 

2. sharex=True, sharey=True
    a. Shared X-axis
    b. Shared Y-axis 

3. ax.set_title(), fig.suptitle()
    a. Subplot Titles
    b. Figure Title

4. plt.subplots()
    a. Object-Oriented
    b. Preffered Method

5. plt.tight_layout()
    a. Automatic Adjustment
    b. Overlap Fix

6. ax.text(), ax.annotate()
    a. Text Addition
    b. Annotation Insertion

7. fig.subplots_adjust()
    a. Manual Adjustment
    b. Spacing Control
